About Me

Glenn is a mechanical engineer and a self-taught software developer. His multidisciplinary background helps him understand the needs and challenges that people of different backgrounds face. This makes him an excellent developer, problem solver, communicator and team-player.

Background:

Glenn started out as a mechanical engineer, but recently been more involved with software development projects. Glenn loves tinker with things and learn about new technologies. While his python is the programing language he has most experice with, he well leap at the opertunity to learn new tools. Having used mypy and pytest in larger projects, he knows the value automated testing. Glenn favorite language is rust, a language with emphasis on strong typing, correctness and memory safety.
