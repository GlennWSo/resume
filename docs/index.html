About Me

Glenn is a passionate software developer, he even codes electric sheep to fall asleep. His multidisciplinary background helps him understand the needs and challenges that people of different backgrounds face. This makes him an excellent developer, tech-lead and team-player.

For the impatient reader:

He can help you with anything from writing firmware in Rust to machine learning in Python. Glenn has great math skills and uses those to solve problems related to 3D graphics, modeling and physics simulations.

Background:

He started his carrier as mechanical engineer, but has now changed tracks to software development. The reason behind this change was simple, he loves to create and to iterate fast! Glenn found his love for programing when he studied optimization and automation of mechanical design. Now his strongest language is Python, but he prefers to use Rust whenever possible.
